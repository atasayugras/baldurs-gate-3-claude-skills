# bg3-report - Anti-patterns

- DO NOT skip the context questions - act, party, and build are required for a useful recommendation
- DO NOT web-fetch item properties - read `../bg3-items/references/items.md` first
- DO NOT give a "keep maybe" verdict - always commit to one of the six verdicts (EQUIP/CAMP/COMPANION/SELL/QUEST/INVESTIGATE)
- DO NOT recommend selling quest items - always check `../bg3-quest/references/quests.md`
- DO NOT recommend selling items that appear in any build in `../bg3-build/references/builds.md`
- DO NOT guess item stats - if it's not in the references, ask the player to describe the properties
- DO NOT skip the achievement check for boss kills and major quest decisions
