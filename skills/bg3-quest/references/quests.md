## DETAILED WALKTHROUGHS

### Rescue the Grand Duke
**Act:** 1 → 3 | **Type:** Side (achievement-adjacent)

**Overview:** Grand Duke Ravengard was captured by Absolute forces. Multi-act rescue chain.

**Steps:**
- Act 1: Rescue Counsellor Florrick from Waukeen's Rest burning inn. Learn the Duke's fate.
- Act 2: Witness Ravengard being tadpoled at Moonrise Towers.
- Act 3 (Wyrm's Rock): Attend Gortash's investiture ceremony with Wyll in party. After ceremony, Mizora arrives at camp.
  - Choose "Break Wyll's pact" → **Loophole achievement unlocked**
  - Ravengard is in Iron Throne (Gortash's underwater prison) — rescue him there.

**Critical:** Wyll must be in your active party at the investiture ceremony.

**Reward:** Florrick rescued = one of three lightning weapons (Joltshooter / Sparky Points / Spellsparkler). Ravengard becomes an Act 3 ally.

**Missable:** If you trigger Waukeen's Rest then long rest or fast travel before rescuing Florrick, she dies permanently.

### Find Dribbles the Clown
**Act:** 3 | **Type:** Side

**Overview:** After defeating the doppelganger Dribbles at Circus of the Last Days, Ringmaster Lucretious asks you to find 7 body parts of the real Dribbles.

**Quest start:** Talk to Lucretious at the Circus after the doppelganger fight.

**7 Body Parts:**

| Part | Location | Coordinates | Notes |
|------|----------|-------------|-------|
| Severed Hand | Circus interior, Popper's shop | X: -89, Y: -52 | Buy for 100-10,000g or steal |
| Severed Torso | Temple Cave below | X: 63, Y: -1028 | Kill doppelganger Muzz/Rudd/Zomm first |
| Severed Arm | Peartree House basement | X: 32, Y: -96 | SE of Sorcerous Sundries; forced entry = Flaming Fist hostile |
| Severed Leg | Lavernica's Home basement | X: 793, Y: 980 | Lower City, SW of Central Wall waypoint |
| Severed Foot | Rainforest's Home basement | X: -93, Y: -109 | Between Beehive General Goods and The Bibliophile |
| Severed Pelvis | Empty residence, Lower City | X: 123, Y: 4 | NW of Basilisk Gate Barracks |
| Severed Head | Undercity Ruins | X: -65, Y: 1041 | Attack hanging corpses nearby to open the door |

**Reward:** Spellmight Gloves (Very Rare — great for Gale). Dribbles is revived, Circus leaves the city.

### Solve the Open Hand Temple Murders
**Act:** 3 | **Type:** Side (required for certain achievements / Lower City access)

**Overview:** Investigate the deaths of Baba Lorgan and Brilgor. Investigator Valeria closed the case too quickly.

**Steps:**
1. Talk to Sister Yannis (X: -44, Y: -6)
2. Get wound info from Sister Rose (cut to bone, poison used)
3. Dig up Brilgor's grave (X: -106, Y: -19, behind temple) — Speak with Dead
4. Enter secret tunnel through kitchen hatch (X: -62, Y: 3)
5. Kill 3 doppelganger assassins — read Field Report to learn "Dolor"
6. Take Stillmaker dagger (murder weapon)
7. Flowery Key opens Fraygo's Flophouse (upper floor, X: -6, Y: 72)
8. Find Ffion Goldgrind's body — Dolor is her son
9. Take Bloodstained Parchment (victim list, Bhaalist connection)
10. Present dagger + parchment to Valeria at Sharess' Caress

**Reward:** Lower City Pass (access to restricted areas).

**WARNING:** If you rescue Valeria BEFORE completing this quest and reporting your findings, she is permanently lost — which affects the Murder Tribunal chain.

### Help the Spirit of the Amulet
**Act:** 1 → 3 | **Type:** Side

**Overview:** A sentient amulet contains a monk spirit who wants to meet his descendant.

**Act 1:** Pick up Sentient Amulet at Adamantine Forge (X: -570, Y: 229).

**Act 3:**
1. Go to Open Hand Temple (Wyrm's Crossing)
2. Learn Shirra Clarwen has died
3. Find her grave in the temple crypt
4. Decision:
   - **Accept the curse** (2× DC 15 WIS/CON): Gain Tasha's Hideous Laughter 1/Long Rest
   - **Refuse the curse**: Fight Monk's Manifestation + 3 Greater Zombies → Sentient Amulet upgrades to Very Rare

**Approval:** Accepting the curse: Halsin, Karlach, Minsc, Wyll +approval. Refusing: Lae'zel, Gale +approval.

### Find Mol
**Act:** 2 → 3 | **Type:** Side

**Overview:** Tiefling gang leader Mol was captured. Find her in Act 3.

**Guildhall access options:**
- Nearby manhole + break wall + DC 20 Sleight of Hand
- Complete Aid the Underduke — guard lets you pass
- **Jaheira in active party = immediate access**

**Steps:**
1. Talk to Mol in Guildhall
2. Give her Mol's Eyepatch
3. (Optional) Retrieve Mol's infernal contract from House of Hope (DC 10 WIS + DC 20 Arcana on Infernal Gem)
4. Give contract to Mol → DC 15 Persuasion → Mol joins as ally in final battle

**Reward:** Mol becomes a "Gather Your Allies" quest ally in the final battle.

### Investigate the Suspicious Toys
**Act:** 3 | **Type:** Side

**Overview:** Explosive toys are being distributed to refugees. Trace the chain to Banite cultists.

**Steps:**
1. Arfur's Mansion (X: 11, Y: -95) — find letter in basement
2. Report to Manip Nestor → disarm explosive toy bears in Requisitioned Barn
3. Confront Arfur Gregorio (DC 15 Intimidation → get passphrase + coronation pass)
   - If he flees: find him at Sharess' Caress (X: 22, Y: 75)
4. Felogyr's Fireworks (X: 12, Y: -125) — passphrase: **"Uncle Felogyr sent me"**
5. Kill Banite cultists on the upper floor

**Key decision:** Leave Arfur alive to receive Wyrm's Rock coronation pass.

**Note:** The final fight cannot be stealthed out of — combat is mandatory.

### Free the Artist
**Act:** 1 → 3 | **Type:** Side

**Overview:** Painter Oskar is held captive by the Zhentarim. Free him in Act 1, then help break a curse on him in Act 3.

**Act 1:** Zhentarim Hideout west of Waukeen's Rest (barn, X: -113, Y: 613). Buy Oskar from Brem (1,000g, bargain to 600g) or persuade.

**Act 3:**
1. Find Oskar enchanted at Lady Jannath's Estate
2. Punch him (don't kill him) — breaks the immediate grip
3. Combine Oskar's Portrait with an empty easel in the Atelier (upstairs)
4. Get Torch of Revocation: buy from Mystic Carrion at Philgrave's Mansion, complete their quest, or loot from their body
5. Destroy the cursed portrait
6. Resolve Kerri Evenfield's spirit: Intimidation or Persuasion DC 15-18

**Reward:** Oskar paints a portrait (worth 1,500g). Till Death Do Us Part + After Death Do Us Part rings.
