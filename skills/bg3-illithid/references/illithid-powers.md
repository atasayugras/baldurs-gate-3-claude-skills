# BG3 - Illithid Powers Reference

Playthrough-agnostic reference. All 25 powers, mechanics, tadpole economy, and Cull the Weak table.

---

## MECHANIC OVERVIEW

- Total powers: **25** — Inner Ring (5) + Middle Ring (10) + Outer Ring (10)
- Each power costs 1 normal tadpole
- **Outer Ring** requires the Astral-Touched Tadpole first (obtained from the Emperor in Act 2 before the Ketheric finale)
- Astral-Touched Tadpole: automatically unlocks the full Inner Ring (5 powers) + Fly, and refunds any tadpoles spent on Inner Ring powers (up to 5 refunded)

- Inner Ring (5 powers) + Fly unlock for free

## INNER RING — 5 Powers (free via Astral-Touched Tadpole)

| Power | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Concentrated Blast** | Action: Deal 3d6 Psychic damage while you have a Concentration spell active |
| **Favourable Beginnings** | Passive: Your first attack roll or ability check against each target gets +Proficiency Bonus |
| **Force Tunnel** | Charge action: Push everything in your path 4m |
| **Psionic Overload** | Passive: Attacks deal +1d4 Psychic, but you also take 1d4 Psychic each turn |
| **Transfuse Health** | Action: Sacrifice half your max HP to heal a target |

---

## MIDDLE RING — 10 Powers (1 tadpole each)

| Power | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Stage Fright** | Passive: Enemies who attack you get Disadvantage + take 2d6 Psychic on a miss |
| **Ability Drain** | Passive: Hits reduce target's relevant ability score by 1 |
| **Luck of the Far Realms** | Reaction (1/Long Rest): Convert any hit into a critical hit |
| **Charm** | Reaction: When attacked, attempt to Charm the attacker (WIS save) |
| **Displace** | Passive: Targets you push or knock fall deal +1d8 Psychic |
| **Repulsor** | Action: Push target 6m + deal 2d6 Force damage |
| **Psionic Backlash** | Reaction: When an ally is hit by a spell, deal the caster spell-level × 1d4 Psychic |
| **Cull the Weak** | Passive: Enemies with HP lower than your unlocked Illithid power count instantly die when damaged |
| **Shield of Thralls** | Bonus Action: Grant 10 Temporary HP to a target |
| **Perilous Stakes** | Action: Target takes double damage from all sources, but attackers heal when hitting it |

---

## OUTER RING — 10 Powers (Astral-Touched Tadpole + 1 tadpole each)

| Power | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Fly** | **Free with Astral-Touched Tadpole.** Fly at your movement speed. |
| **Fracture Psyche** | Passive: Hits reduce target's AC by 1 (stacks) |
| **Illithid Expertise** | Passive: Expertise in Persuasion, Deception, and Intimidation |
| **Psionic Dominance** | Reaction: If a spell targeting you has a level ≤ your Proficiency Bonus, you can negate it |
| **Black Hole** | Action: Create a gravity point that pulls enemies in and slows them |
| **Mind Blast** | Action: Cone attack — 4d8+mod Psychic, potentially Stunned |
| **Mind Sanctuary** | Action: You can use your Action and Bonus Action interchangeably this turn |
| **Freecast** | 1/Long Rest: Cast one spell without spending a spell slot |
| **Absorb Intellect** | Action: Target loses 1 INT per turn for 5 turns, you heal 1d8 HP per turn |
| **Displacer Beast Shape** | Action: Transform into a Displacer Beast (85 HP) |

## CULL THE WEAK — THRESHOLD TABLE

Threshold = number of Illithid powers currently unlocked. Enemies at or below that HP die instantly when damaged.

| Stage | Powers Unlocked | Kill Threshold |
|-------|----------------|----------------|
| Early game (0 unlocked) | 0 | 0 HP |
| Inner Ring only (5) | 5 | 5 HP |
| Middle Ring partial (7) | 7 | 7 HP |
| Full Inner + Middle (15) | 15 | 15 HP |
| After Astral-Touched Tadpole (all Inner free + Fly) | 16 | 16 HP |
| Full unlock (25) | 25 | 25 HP |

## BUILD-SPECIFIC UNLOCK PRIORITY

### For Darkness / Bladelock (Warlock/Fighter melee)

**Outer Ring priority:**
1. **Freecast** — Cast Darkness or Armor of Agathys without spending a slot (1/Long Rest)
2. **Mind Sanctuary** — Use Action and Bonus Action interchangeably; with Action Surge this is devastating
3. **Fracture Psyche** — Extra Attack + Action Surge = -5 AC in one turn on a boss
4. **Mind Blast** — Blind enemies in Darkness with a Psychic cone + potential Stun
5. **Fly** — Free, take it
6. **Black Hole** — Position control

**Middle Ring priority:**
1. **Luck of the Far Realms** — Convert a hit to crit 1/Long Rest
2. **Cull the Weak** — Threshold scales with unlock count
3. **Psionic Backlash** — Punish casters
4. **Shield of Thralls** — 10 Temp HP is reliable sustain
5. **Stage Fright** — In Darkness, enemies already miss often + 2d6 Psychic on miss

### For Sorcadin / Spellcaster

**Outer Ring priority:**
1. **Freecast** — Free high-level spell 1/Long Rest
2. **Psionic Dominance** — Negate spells targeting you
3. **Illithid Expertise** — Social skill expertise (frees up spell/feat slots)
4. **Mind Sanctuary** — Doubles casting options in a turn

**Middle Ring priority:**
1. **Luck of the Far Realms** — Auto-crit with a high-damage spell
2. **Psionic Backlash** — Punish enemy casters
3. **Cull the Weak** — Passive add-on once threshold is high enough
