## Key Rules

- Inner Ring: 5 powers, 1 tadpole each (Astral-Touched Tadpole refunds these and unlocks them for free)
- Middle Ring: 10 powers, 1 tadpole each
- Outer Ring: 10 powers, 1 tadpole each — requires Astral-Touched Tadpole to unlock the ring
- Cull the Weak threshold = number of Illithid powers currently unlocked
- Freecast and Mind Sanctuary are Outer Ring premium picks for almost all builds
