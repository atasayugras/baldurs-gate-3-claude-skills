# BG3 - Items Reference

Playthrough-independent item reference. Named items organized by type, with properties explained in plain language.

---

## STANDARD WEAPON PROPERTIES

| Property | Meaning |
|----------|---------|
| Finesse | Use STR or DEX, whichever is higher. Required for Rogue Sneak Attack. |
| Thrown | Melee weapon can be thrown as ranged. Uses STR modifier + proficiency. |
| Two-Handed | Requires both hands. |
| Light | Can dual-wield with another Light weapon. |
| Heavy | Heavy weapon. |
| Versatile | One or two hands; two hands deal more damage (e.g., 1d10/1d8). |
| Extra Reach | Melee range extended from 1.5m to 2.5m. |
| Enchantment (+1/+2/+3) | Bonus to attack rolls and damage; on armor, increases AC. |

## NAMED WEAPONS

### Phalar Aluve (Underdark - stone at X:116 Y:-192)
- Rare longsword +1, Versatile, Finesse, 1d8+1 / 1d10+1 slashing
- **Phalar Aluve: Melody (1/Short Rest):** Choose Shriek or Sing
  - **Shriek:** Nearby enemies -1d4 attack + saves (like Bane)
  - **Sing:** Nearby allies +1d4 attack + saves (like Bless) + Thunder/Psychic resistance
- **Why it matters:** The Shriek/Sing aura benefits your whole party, not just the wielder. One of the best support weapons in the game.

### Knife of the Undermountain King (Creche Y'llek - A'jak'nir Jeera, 1392g)
- Very Rare shortsword +2, Finesse, Light, 1d6+2 piercing
- **Organ Rearranger:** Critical hit threshold 19 (not 20). If you roll 2 or fewer damage, reroll and take the higher result.
- **Shadow Blade:** Advantage on attacks vs Obscured targets
- **Best in slot:** Best Act 1/2 shortsword; pairs extremely well with Darkness builds (crit on 19 while in Darkness).

### Blood of Lathander (Creche Y'llek - secret chamber)
- Legendary mace, 1d6+3 bludgeoning
- **Lathander's Blessing:** Nearby allies cannot be reduced below 1 HP once per long rest; at 0 they revive with 2d6 HP
- **Lathander's Light:** Blinds nearby Undead and Fiends automatically; toggleable
- **Best for:** Shadowheart; ideal in Act 2 undead-heavy areas.

### Mourning Frost (Underdark - combine Icy Crystal + Icy Helve + Icy Metal)
- Very Rare quarterstaff +1, Versatile, 1d6+1 / 1d8+1 bludgeoning + 1d4 cold
- **Heart of Ice:** Dealing Cold damage adds +1 Cold damage extra
- **Insidious Cold:** Cold spell damage inflicts Chilled
- Provides Ray of Frost cantrip at-will
- **Best for:** Gale; foundation of a frost build.

### Sussur Greatsword (Blighted Village forge - standard greatsword + Sussur Bark)
- Rare greatsword +1, Two-Handed, 2d6+1 slashing
- **On Hit:** DC 12 CON save or 2 turns Silenced (blocks verbal spell components)
- **Best for:** Lae'zel against enemy casters; swap it in specifically for caster fights.

### Faithbreaker (Dror Ragzlin drop - Shattered Sanctum)
- Uncommon +1 Versatile warhammer, 1d8+1 (one-hand) / 1d10+1 (two-hand) + STR, +1d6 force
- **Absolute Power:** Force damage + 5m push (action)
- **Best Act 1 weapon for Warlock/Paladin builds** (scales with CHA via Pact of the Blade).

### Everburn Blade (Zhalk drop - Prologue)
- Uncommon greatsword, 2d6 slashing + 1d4 fire, two-handed
- Always on fire; ignores non-magical slashing resistance.

### Selune's Spear of Night (Dame Aylin gift - Act 3 camp, after Ketheric defeat)
- Legendary spear +3, Versatile, Thrown
- **Moonbeam (1/Long Rest):** Level 2 spell
- **Darkvision:** 12m
- **Advantage on Wisdom Saving Throws + Perception Checks**
- **How to get:** Spare the Nightsong in Act 2 → defeat Ketheric → first long rest in Act 3 → Dame Aylin appears at camp → talk to her.
- Do NOT kill the Nightsong. You lose this upgrade AND the Leave No One Behind achievement.

### Stillmaker (Open Hand Temple Murders - murder weapon)
- Very Rare +2 dagger, Finesse, Light, Thrown, 1d4+2 Piercing
- **Hold Person (Level 3, 1/Long Rest):** Paralyzes a humanoid → all melee attacks auto-crit
- **WARNING:** This is the murder weapon in the Open Hand Temple Murders quest. Do not hand it to Valeria or Devella before the quest is fully resolved.

### Harmonic Dueller (Entharl Danthelon - Wyrm's Crossing)
- Rare +1 rapier, Finesse, 1d8+1 Piercing
- **Mellow Harmony (1/Short Rest):** DC 15 Performance check → all melee attacks deal +CHA modifier bonus damage for the turn. Ends if rapier is unequipped.
- **Synergy:** Pact of the Blade (CHA to attack+damage) + Ring of Arcane Synergy (+CHA from conditions) + Mellow Harmony (+CHA) = triple CHA stack.

### Spear of Night (Gauntlet of Shar - Silent Library hidden room)
- Rare spear +1, Versatile, Thrown
- **Shar's Blessing:** Required for Shadowheart's Chosen of Shar quest at the Nightsong confrontation
- **Upgrades to Selune's Spear of Night (Legendary +3) if you spare the Nightsong.**

### Titanstring Bow (Lann Tarv - Moonrise Towers / Brem - Zhentarim Hideout, 310g)
- Rare longbow +1, Two-Handed, 1d8+1 piercing, 18m range
- **Titan Weapon:** Adds STR modifier as bonus damage to every attack
- Uses DEX for attack rolls, STR for damage. With STR 20+, adds +5 damage per arrow.

## NOTABLE ARMOR

### Adamantine Scale Mail / Splint Armor (Adamantine Forge craft)
- Heavy armor (crafted from Mithral Ore at Grymforge)
- **Critical hit immunity:** Enemies cannot critically hit you
- **Adamantine Backlash:** Melee attacker gets 2 turns Reeling (-1 attack/turn)
- Scale Mail: 16 AC | Splint Armor: 17 AC
- **Best defensive armor in Acts 1-2.**

### Luminous Armour (Act 2 vendor/loot)
- Uncommon medium armor, 15 AC
- **Radiating Shockwave:** Dealing Radiant damage creates a Radiating Orb on the target (-1 attack per turn)
- **Best for:** Shadowheart with Blood of Lathander - the radiant aura automatically applies Radiating Orbs every turn.

### Dark Justiciar Helmet - Very Rare (Gauntlet of Shar - Silent Library gilded chest, X:-822 Y:-753)
- Very Rare helmet
- **Magical Durability:** +1 to Saving Throws vs spells
- **Covert Critical:** While Obscured, critical hit threshold -1
- **Darkness build BiS:** In Darkness = constantly Obscured → with Knife of Undermountain King (crit on 19) = crits on 18.

### Grymskull Helm (Grym drop - Adamantine Forge)
- Very Rare helmet, requires Heavy Armour proficiency
- **Critical hit immunity** (enemies cannot crit you)
- **Fire resistance**
- **Hunter's Mark (1/Long Rest):** +1d6 damage per hit on marked target

### Flawed Helldusk Armour / Gloves / Helmet (Dammon craft - 1 Infernal Iron)
- Heavy armor set (crafted)
- Helmet: **+2 bonus to Saving Throws vs spells**
- Gloves: **+1d4 Fire damage on weapon attacks; +1d4 Necrotic + possible Bleeding on unarmed**
- Armor: **Melee attackers within 2m take 1-4 Fire damage; -1 incoming Piercing damage**

### Icebite Robe (Mason Guild Graveyard, Left Sarcophagus, X:-157 Y:72)
- Rare clothing
- **Cold resistance**
- **Armour of Agathys (Level 3, 1/Long Rest):** 15 Temp HP + 15 Cold retaliation (no concentration)
- **Best for:** Gale or any caster who needs free HP buffer.

## NOTABLE ACCESSORIES

### Spellcrux Amulet (Moonrise Towers Prison - The Warden drop)
- Very Rare amulet
- **Spell Slot Restoration (Bonus Action, 1/Long Rest):** Recover any spent spell slot
- **Why Bonus Action matters:** You can recover a slot AND cast a spell in the same turn.

### Surgeon's Subjugation Amulet (Malus Thorm drop - House of Healing)
- Rare amulet
- **Paralysing Critical (1/Long Rest):** On a critical hit vs a humanoid, optionally apply 2 turns of Paralyze
- Paralyzed: Incapacitated + Vulnerable to physical damage (2x) + auto-hit + auto-crit from within 3m

### Strange Conduit Ring (Creche Y'llek - Inquisitor's Chamber, 40g)
- Uncommon ring
- **Strange Conduit:** While concentrating on a spell, weapon attacks deal +1d4 Psychic damage
- Ideal for Darkness builds: Darkness requires concentration → free +1d4 on every hit for zero cost.

### Ring of Arcane Synergy (Act 2 vendor/loot)
- Rare ring
- **Arcane Synergy:** When you apply a condition to an enemy, gain 2 turns of Arcane Synergy = weapon attacks deal +spellcasting modifier damage
- **Bladelock synergy:** Darkness inflicts Blinded → Arcane Synergy → +CHA modifier per hit.

### The Whispering Promise (Volo - Emerald Grove)
- Uncommon ring
- **Promising Presence:** When you heal another creature, they gain 2 turns of Bless (+1d4 to attack rolls + saves)
- **For healers:** Mass Healing Word + Whispering Promise = entire party gets Bless simultaneously.

### Killer's Sweetheart (Act 2/3 loot)
- Very Rare ring
- **Executioner (1/Long Rest):** After killing an enemy, your next attack roll is automatically a critical hit
- **Nova builds:** Use the guaranteed crit with Divine Smite or Eldritch Smite for massive burst.

### Shadow Blade Ring (Arabella quest reward - Act 2)
- Rare ring
- **Shadow Blade (Bonus Action, 1/Short Rest):** Creates a Shadow Blade weapon (2d8 Psychic, Finesse, Light; no concentration)
- **Can be made a Pact Weapon:** Warlock Pact of the Blade works with it, using CHA modifier to attack.
- Psychic damage bypasses most resistances.

### Shield of Devotion (Quartermaster Talli - Last Light Inn, 570g)
- Very Rare shield
- **Aid (Level 3, 1/Long Rest):** Self-only version; heals and increases HP cap
- +2 AC (standard shield)

### Gloves of the Duellist (Lann Tarv - Moonrise Towers, 160g)
- Rare gloves
- **Duelling Gloves:** +2 to melee weapon Attack Rolls when wielding a one-handed weapon with an empty or shield offhand
- Does NOT require free offhand - shields are fine. Only deactivates if holding weapon two-handed.

### Gauntlets of the Warmaster (Entharl Danthelon - Wyrm's Crossing, ~1600g)
- Very Rare gloves, requires Medium Armour proficiency
- **+1 Attack Rolls**
- **Heightened Manoeuvre:** All Battle Master maneuvers and weapon actions impose Disadvantage on the target's saving throw
- One of the most expensive gloves in the game; makes every Trip Attack, Menacing Attack, etc. reliably land.

### Disintegrating Night Walkers (Grymforge - Nere drop)
- Story Item boots
- **Misty Step (Bonus Action, 1/Short Rest):** Teleport up to 18m
- **Night Walker:** Immunity to Enwebbed/Entangled/Ensnared; no slipping on Grease or ice

### Boots of Striding (Shattered Sanctum - Minthara)
- Uncommon boots, Athletics +1, requires Medium Armour proficiency
- **Focused Stride:** When casting a Concentration spell: gain 1 turn of Momentum + Prone immunity + immunity to forced movement
- **For concentration builds:** Casting Darkness activates all three protections simultaneously.
