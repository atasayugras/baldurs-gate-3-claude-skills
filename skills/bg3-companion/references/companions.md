## ASTARION

**Quest:** The Pale Elf → Open Your Scars → Servant of None
**Acts:** 1-3

**Quest Overview:**
- Act 1: Astarion's dark nature is revealed. He asks to bite you at night.
- Act 2: Learn his history with Cazador.
- Act 3: Cazador's Palace — choice to let Astarion ascend or stop him.

**Key Decision:** At Cazador's Palace, Astarion can Ascend (absorbing 7,000 vampire spawn) or become a free vampire. Ascending makes him more powerful but changes his personality.

**Achievement:** **Just a Nibble** — Let him bite you at night when he asks (Act 1, at camp).

**Romance:**
1. Approve of his actions in early Act 1
2. He asks to bite you — allow it (also unlocks achievement)
3. Night conversation triggers around approval 30+
4. Act 2: deepen the relationship through camp dialogues
5. Act 3: romance scene before Cazador quest

**High Approval:** Ruthless/selfish choices, letting him feed, supporting his self-interest
**Low Approval:** Moralizing, preventing him from feeding, self-sacrifice choices

**Missable:** The Act 3 ascension choice cannot be undone.

## GALE

**Quest:** The Wizard of Waterdeep → In Case of Death... → The Prince of the Comet
**Acts:** 1-3

**Quest Overview:**
- Act 1: Gale reveals his Netherese orb condition — must periodically feed it with magic items.
- Act 2: Visit Gale's tower (via his scroll) and meet a sage contact. Required for Repairing the Weave.
- Act 3: Orb resolution — multiple ending options (self-sacrifice, Wish, etc.)

**Achievement:** **Repairing the Weave** — Complete the tower visit in Act 2 and meet the contact there.

**Approval Tips:**
**Gains:** Intellectual curiosity, knowledge-seeking, showing kindness, good moral choices
**Losses:** Destruction of magic items (ironic), cruelty, reckless magic use

**Romance:**
1. Feed his orb with magic items when asked (don't let him go too long without)
2. Approval 25+: romantic camp dialogue options appear
3. Act 2: visit his tower together
4. Act 3: commitment scene available

**Note:** Gale can sacrifice himself to destroy the Netherbrain if you fail to find another solution. His death is not permanent if you've progressed his questline.

## SHADOWHEART

**Quest:** Daughter of Darkness → The Chosen of Shar → Investigate the House of Grief
**Acts:** 1-3

**Quest Overview:**
- Act 1: Shadowheart's devotion to Shar is a mystery — do not press too hard early.
- Act 2: The Gauntlet of Shar. Major decision: spare or kill the Nightsong (Dame Aylin). **NEVER kill the Nightsong** — it breaks the Spear of Night legendary upgrade and the Leave No One Behind achievement chain.
- Act 3: House of Grief. Confrontation with Viconia DeVir and Shadowheart's parents.

**Achievement:** **To Bloom in Darkest Night** — Give Shadowheart a Night Orchid found in the Act 2 shadow lands.

**Key Item:** Spear of Night (Act 2, Silent Library). If you spare the Nightsong, this upgrades to Selune's Spear of Night (Legendary +3) after the first Act 3 long rest.

**Romance:**
1. Support her faith early (don't mock Shar)
2. After the Nightsong decision, check in at camp
3. A romance scene becomes available after surviving the Ketheric fight
4. Act 3: deepens through House of Grief quest

**Approval Tips:**
**Gains:** Cruelty that serves a purpose, pragmatism, following her lead on Shar matters
**Losses:** Openly worshipping other gods, pressing her on her past too hard early

## LAE'ZEL

**Quest:** The Githyanki Warrior → Vlaakith's Will → Help Kith'rak Voss
**Acts:** 1-3

**Quest Overview:**
- Act 1: Lae'zel wants to reach the Githyanki Creche for a cure.
- Act 2: Voss appears — he's hunting Lae'zel to prevent Vlaakith from getting the Githyanki weapon.
- Act 3: The path to freeing Orpheus and the Githyanki political situation resolves.

**Approval Tips:**
**Gains:** Strength, directness, martial excellence, Githyanki culture respect, ruthless decisions
**Losses:** Mercy, weakness, worshipping non-Githyanki gods, cowardice

**Romance:**
1. Act 1: She offers a challenge fight — engage it
2. Approve of her Githyanki values consistently
3. Night dialogue options open around approval 30+
4. Act 2-3: relationship evolves with the Orpheus storyline

## WYLL

**Quest:** The Blade of Frontiers → The Grand Duke
**Acts:** 1-3

**ACHIEVEMENT: Loophole** — Break Wyll's pact with Mizora.

**How to get Loophole:**
1. Wyll must be in your **active party** at Gortash's investiture ceremony (Act 3, Wyrm's Rock)
2. Let the ceremony complete (don't attack Gortash during it)
3. After the ceremony, Mizora appears at camp
4. Select **"Break Wyll's pact"** (not "accept the terms")
5. Wyll becomes a free mortal (his father is saved separately via Iron Throne)

**Quest Overview:**
- Act 1: Wyll hunts devils. His patron Mizora controls him.
- Act 2: Mizora is captured — you can free or leave her.
- Act 3: Ravengard (Wyll's father) is in Iron Throne. Rescue him. The pact can be broken.

**Note:** Also need to have spoken with Gortash for the Loophole dialogue to trigger.

**Approval Tips:**
**Gains:** Heroism, protecting the innocent, honor, good moral choices
**Losses:** Cruelty, abandoning allies, self-serving decisions

## KARLACH

**Quest:** Our Fiery Friend → The Hellion's Heart → Forging a Heart → Fulfill the Deal
**Acts:** 1-3

**ACHIEVEMENT: Hot Date** — Invite Karlach for a special evening after repairing her infernal engine for the second time.

**Engine Repairs:**
1. **Act 1:** Dammon at Last Light Inn (Emerald Grove initially, but he moves to Act 2). Bring 1 Infernal Iron. Engine cooled — now she can touch people.
2. **Act 3:** Dammon at Forge of the Nine (Lower City). Bring 2nd Infernal Iron. Second repair done.

**Hot Date unlock:** The evening after the second repair, invite Karlach to camp (dialogue option at her tent).

**Approval Tips:**
**Gains:** Justice, fighting for the weak, enthusiasm, kindness, calling out hypocrisy
**Losses:** Cruelty, working with Zariel, letting Wyll suffer under his pact

**Note:** Karlach's Act 3 ending is bittersweet by default — her engine has a limited lifespan. Additional options may exist depending on quest completion.

## HALSIN

**Quest:** Save the First Druid → Lift the Shadow Curse (Act 2)
**Acts:** 1-3 (joins camp Act 2, active party Act 3)

**Key Role:** The Shadow Curse lift in Act 2 requires his involvement. Bring him along for that quest.

**Note:** Halsin joins the active party later than other companions. He's primarily an Act 3 companion.

**Approval Tips:**
**Gains:** Nature respect, protecting innocents, opposing the Absolute, environmental care
**Losses:** Cruelty, burning things, harming nature

## MINTHARA

**Recruitment:** Only available if you side with the Goblins (raid Emerald Grove) in Act 1. This closes off Halsin, the Tieflings, and several achievements including **Leave No One Behind**. Not compatible with 100% achievement runs.

**Note:** She can be non-lethally knocked out in Act 1 (True Soul dialogue option) and recruited in Act 2 at Moonrise without siding with goblins — but this is a specific early exploit. Check current patch behavior.

---

## JAHEIRA

**Recruit:** Act 2, Last Light Inn. She's already there when you arrive.

**Quest:** The High Harper (Act 3 — involves tracking Minsc)

**Key benefit:** Jaheira in active party = free access to Guildhall (Find Mol quest).

---

## MINSC

**Recruit:** Act 3, Lower City — reached through Jaheira's High Harper quest.

**Note:** Jaheira must be in your party to recruit Minsc. He comes late but adds a strong frontline fighter.
