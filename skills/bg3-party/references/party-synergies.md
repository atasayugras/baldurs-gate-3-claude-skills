# BG3 - Party Composition Guide

Party archetypes, companion build recommendations for each Main build, and synergy patterns.

---

## PARTY DESIGN PRINCIPLES

Every party needs:
1. **Frontline** — Someone who can take hits and hold ground
2. **Sustained damage** — Reliable DPS every turn
3. **AoE or control** — Clearing groups or locking down priority targets
4. **Utility** — Out-of-combat skills (Perception, Persuasion, lockpicking, medicine), plus in-combat utility (healing, buff, repositioning)

BG3 party size: Main + 3 companions.

---

## PARTY ARCHETYPES

### Darkness Party (Highest Tactician/Honor Mode Ceiling)
**Concept:** Everyone operates inside or around Darkness. Main and select companions have Devil's Sight, others use ranged and AOE from outside.

**Composition:**
- **Main:** Bladelock (Warlock/Fighter) — Darkness engine, melee DPS
- **Companion 1: Lae'zel** (Fighter Battle Master) — Second frontline. Phalar Aluve aura applies even in Darkness. Doesn't need sight — just hits things in melee.
- **Companion 2: Shadowheart** (Cleric) — Stays outside Darkness for Spirit Guardians. Blood of Lathander provides passive Radiant AoE and anti-undead utility.
- **Companion 3: Gale** (Evocation Wizard) — AoE from outside Darkness. Sculpt Spells protects allies from Fireball/Ice Storm.

**Combat Loop:**
1. Turn 1: Main casts Darkness on self or position. Lae'zel enters melee. Shadowheart casts Spirit Guardians outside Darkness. Gale casts AoE.
2. Sustained: Main + Lae'zel with full Advantage vs. Disadvantage for enemies. Spirit Guardians deal Radiant to anything that walks near Shadowheart. Gale keeps concentrating on longer control spells.

**Strength:** Virtually guaranteed hit rate, enemies struggle to target Main. High sustained damage.
**Weakness:** Setup-dependent. Enemies with Truesight ignore Darkness. Relies on Darkness not being dispelled.

---

### Nova (Burst) Party
**Concept:** Concentrate overwhelming damage into 1-2 turns to kill priority targets before they act.

**Composition:**
- **Main:** Sorcadin (Paladin/Sorcerer) — Highest single-turn burst
- **Companion 1: Astarion** (Gloomstalker/Assassin) — First-turn burst, surprised enemies = auto-crit
- **Companion 2: Lae'zel** (Battle Master) — Precision Attack + Trip Attack + Extra Attack sustained
- **Companion 3: Shadowheart** (Life Cleric) — Healing Word (Bonus Action) + Bless for accuracy

**Combat Loop:**
1. Sneak attack before combat starts if possible → Astarion's Assassinate crits on surprised targets
2. Turn 1: Main burns Smite slots on crits. Astarion uses Action Surge + first-turn Gloomstalker bonus attack. Lae'zel Precision + Trip.
3. Boss should be very low or dead by turn 2.

**Strength:** Ends fights quickly — limits exposure to enemy legendary actions.
**Weakness:** Resource-intensive. Less effective in multi-wave encounters.

---

### Control + AoE Party
**Concept:** Lock down enemies with control spells, then clean up with AoE damage.

**Composition:**
- **Main:** Divination Wizard or Sorcerer — Primary controller
- **Companion 1: Shadowheart** (Trickery Cleric) — Pass Without Trace for stealth, Silence for enemy casters
- **Companion 2: Gale** (Evocation Wizard) — AoE backup, sculpt protects party
- **Companion 3: Lae'zel or Karlach** — Frontline to take advantage of prone/restrained enemies

**Combat Loop:**
1. Turn 1: Main casts Hypnotic Pattern or Hold Monster on toughest enemies. Shadowheart casts Silence on enemy caster cluster. Gale prepares next-turn AoE.
2. Sustained: Lae'zel/Karlach melee with Advantage vs. restrained/prone targets (guaranteed crits in melee). Gale drops AoE when enemies are clustered.

**Strength:** Consistent. Works on every difficulty. Strong against caster-heavy encounters.
**Weakness:** Concentration spells compete with each other. Enemies with high WIS saves may resist key spells.

## PARTY PILLARS CHECKLIST

Before finalizing a party composition, verify:
- [ ] At least one frontline character who can survive being surrounded
- [ ] At least one source of Bless or equivalent accuracy buff
- [ ] At least one AoE source (Fireball, Ice Storm, Spirit Guardians, Whirlwind)
- [ ] At least one Bonus Action heal (Healing Word, potion, Shield of Thralls)
- [ ] Out-of-combat: Perception 12+, Persuasion on Main or one companion, lockpicking
