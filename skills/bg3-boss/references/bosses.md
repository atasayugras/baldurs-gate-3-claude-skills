# BG3 Boss Reference

All bosses by act. Each entry: location, key mechanics, immunities/resistances, achievement conditions, strategy.

---

## ACT 1

### Phase Spider Matriarch
**Location:** Whispering Depths (beneath Blighted Village)
**Loot:** Poisoner's Robe

**Key Mechanics:**
- Web floor - restricts movement
- Egg Sacs: destroy them to reduce spider reinforcement spawns
- Short-range phase teleport between platforms

**Strategy:** Destroy Egg Sacs first. Stay mobile and spread out to avoid web clusters.

---

### Auntie Ethel
**Location:** Ancient Abode (beneath Sunlit Wetlands)

**Key Mechanics:**
- Spawns illusion clones - all look identical; any damage reveals fakes (AoE ideal to hit all at once)
- Real Ethel goes Invisible - AoE all clones to find her
- **Below 20% HP:** Triggers bargain dialogue. Accept to receive Auntie Ethel's Hair (+1 to a chosen ability score, permanent)
- **Note:** If you let Ethel escape, she reappears in Act 3 for the Vanra quest

**Achievement-linked:** No direct achievement, but letting her live opens Act 3 content.

**Strategy:** Use AoE on illusion cluster. At 20% HP, choose whether to bargain or kill. The +1 ability score is a permanent buff worth taking if you don't need the kill.

---

### Priestess Gut
**Location:** Goblin Camp - Shattered Sanctum

**Key Mechanics:**
- Can be isolated in her private room
- Attempts to incapacitate via tadpole - accept it to trigger a cutscene, then flee or fight

---

### Dror Ragzlin
**Location:** Goblin Camp - Shattered Sanctum
**Loot:** Faithbreaker (Uncommon warhammer +1, scales with CHA for Pact of the Blade — Act 1 BIS for Bladelock)

**Key Mechanics:**
- Goblin camp boss — goblins, hobgoblins assist
- Connected to spider lair below

---

### Nere
**Location:** Grymforge
**Loot:** Disintegrating Night Walkers (Rare boots - Misty Step 1/Short Rest + immunity to web/entangle)

**Key Mechanics:**
- Can be pressured to release gnomes before combat
- High Psionic damage (Mind Flayer servant)

---

### Grym
**Location:** Adamantine Forge (Grymforge)

**ACHIEVEMENT: A Grym Fate** — Kill Grym without using the forge hammer. Do NOT use the hammer at all.

**Key Mechanics:**
- **Adamantine Skin:** Immune to all damage until superheated by lava
- To superheat: activate the lava valve, pull Grym into the lava
- Once superheated: **Vulnerable to Bludgeoning damage**
- Forge hammer deals 12d8 Bludgeoning (highest source) — using it voids the achievement
- Lava recedes after 2 turns — reactivate valve to maintain superheat
- Magma Mephits may spawn

**Immunities:** All damage (pre-superheat), Fire, Acid (lava creature)

**Strategy (A Grym Fate):**
1. Superheat with lava valve
2. Focus Bludgeoning damage only (Shadowheart mace, Minthara mace, Tremor Bulb grenades)
3. Station one character near the valve — reactivate every 2 turns
4. Ranged characters can attack from above — Grym has no ranged attacks
5. Never activate the forge hammer

---

### Githyanki Inquisitor W'wargaz
**Location:** Creche Y'llek

**Key Mechanics:**
- Switches to Guardian form mid-fight
- Emperor can suppress the Guardian if present
- High bonus vs. Paralyze (Githyanki mental resistance)

**Strategy:** Let Lae'zel speak first to trigger Emperor involvement. Front-load damage before Guardian form activates.

## ACT 2

### Malus Thorm
**Location:** House of Healing

**Key Mechanics:**
- **Easy bypass:** Persuasion/Deception to convince the three Sisters to "practice" on him — they kill him and you still get XP and loot
- Standard combat: nurses heal him — kill nurses first

**Loot:** Surgeon's Subjugation Amulet, Karabasan's Gift x3 (nearby Dolor drop)

---

### Gerringothe Thorm
**Location:** Reithwin Tollhouse

**ACHIEVEMENT: Penny Pincher** — Kill Toll Collector WITHOUT her using her gold ability. Destroy her skeleton skull minions first, or attack before she can act.

**Strategy:** Shoot her through the window before dialogue, or burst her down before she takes a turn. Destroying skeleton heads prevents the gold ability.

---

### Thisobald Thorm
**Location:** The Waning Moon

**ACHIEVEMENT: Bottoms Up** — Kill via the drinking contest (Persuasion chain to keep him drinking until he dies).

**Key Mechanics:**
- Drinking game Persuasion chain — succeed and he dies without combat
- Can also be killed normally, but Bottoms Up requires the drink method

---

### Balthazar
**Location:** Gauntlet of Shar / Shadowfell path

**Key Mechanics:**
- Powerful necromancer, summons undead
- High spell save DC; concentration spells can be disrupted
- Can be encountered early in Gauntlet or again in Shadowfell

**Strategy:** Blood of Lathander radiant blind neutralizes undead summons. Clear summons first, then focus Balthazar. Send full party.

---

### Yurgir the Orthon
**Location:** Gauntlet of Shar
**Loot:** Hellfire Hand Crossbow (Very Rare +2)

**Key Mechanics:**
- Extraplanar bounty hunter — high melee damage
- Contract can be broken via the Lyrthindor rat quest, but there's a Dark Urge bug — may need to fight directly

**Strategy:** Darkness + Devil's Sight gives guaranteed advantage. High burst wins fast.

---

### Ketheric Thorm — Phase 1
**Location:** Moonrise Towers Rooftop

**Key Mechanics:**
- Free the Nightsong (Shadowfell) BEFORE this fight — she removes Ketheric's protection, making him mortal
- Drop HP to ~50 — cutscene triggers, he retreats to the Colony below
- Harper allies assist during the fight

**Strategy:** Free Nightsong first. Rooftop fight is then straightforward DPS — no special mechanic. Follow into Colony for Phase 2.

---

### Apostle of Myrkul (Phase 2 — Ketheric / Act 2 Final Boss)
**Location:** Mind Flayer Colony - Necrotic Laboratory

**ACHIEVEMENT: No Free Lunches** — Kill Apostle before any Necromite reaches and is consumed by him.

**Key Mechanics:**
- 4 Necromites spawn at the room's edges every round
- If a Necromite reaches Myrkul: he casts Finger of Death + heals 8d8 HP (Tactician)
- **Bone Chill aura on the platform:** All characters ON the platform cannot be healed — keep backline OFF the platform
- Passive damage reduction: all incoming damage reduced by 2
- After weapon attack: makes an extra attack

**Immunities:** Disease, form-altering spells (Polymorph, etc.), +5 Banishment saving throw, Charm advantage, magical sleep immunity

**Necromite Vulnerabilities:** Bludgeoning, Radiant
**Necromite Resistances:** Piercing

**Strategy (No Free Lunches):**
- Priority: Necromites first, always
- Gale: Ice Storm on Necromite clusters (Bludgeoning + Cold, both effective)
- Shadowheart: Spirit Guardians / Sacred Flame for Radiant damage on Necromites. Don't heal — Bone Chill on platform blocks it anyway
- Melee character: Mace/Flail (Bludgeoning) on nearby Necromites
- Ranged/Main: Eldritch Blast (Force) on Necromites at range; Force Tunnel (Illithid) to push approaching ones
- Only attack Myrkul when the current round's Necromites are all dead

## ACT 3

### Enver Gortash
**Location:** Wyrm's Rock Fortress - Throne Room

**ACHIEVEMENT: Fancy Footwork** — Kill Gortash without activating ANY trap in his throne room.

**Key Mechanics:**
- 9 Steel Watchers at coronation — fight during coronation is extremely difficult. Fight alone in throne room instead
- Trap types: floor pressure traps, grenade impellers, incineration casters
- **Micromodron Force Curtains:** Give Gortash damage resistance/immunity — destroy them
- **Deranged Force Curtains** (malfunctioning): Give Gortash a DEBUFF — leave these alone
- At 75% HP: Invoke the Black Hand — 150 Temp HP + STR advantage
- +10 Initiative on Tactician — acts first

**Strategy (Fancy Footwork):**
1. Don't attack at coronation — too many enemies
2. Find him alone in throne room later
3. Before engaging: target traps manually (Ctrl + click) and destroy with ranged attacks
4. Leave Deranged curtains intact (they hurt him)
5. High burst damage — he's not particularly durable once traps are cleared

---

### Orin the Red
**Location:** Bhaal Temple - Undercity

**ACHIEVEMENT: First Blood** — Kill Orin WHILE cultist Reapers are actively chanting. The chant must be ongoing at the moment of the killing blow.

**Key Mechanics:**
- Boon of Bhaal: While Reapers chant, Orin gains Unstoppable stacks (Tactician: 12 stacks — each reduces all damage by 1)
- To deal damage through stacks: deplete them first with attacks, OR just ignore stacks and burst through
- If chant stops before kill: Boon of Bhaal ends → achievement void. Reapers must be alive and chanting at kill moment
- Dark Urge: Orin fights in Humanoid form (no Slayer transformation)
- Dual wield: Bloodthirst + second blade
- Murderous Strikes: 5 stacks → Deathbringer Assault (7 attacks)
- Immune: form-altering, +5 Banishment

**Strategy (First Blood):**
- Do NOT kill Reapers — chant must continue
- AoE hits Orin + Reapers simultaneously — fine, just don't kill Reapers
- Deplete Unstoppable stacks with burst damage, then focus Orin
- Deliver killing blow while chant is active — confirm Reapers are alive

---

### Raphael
**Location:** House of Hope - Foyer

**Key Mechanics:**
- 4 Soul Pillars: Each active pillar gives +1d12 Fire damage + +3 DEX saves
- Pillars become vulnerable when combat starts — Radiant damage destroys them fastest
- Ascended form: All fire spells bypass any Fire resistance/immunity
- Action Surge: Uses Multiattack twice per turn
- Killing blow may prevent reviving downed characters

**Strategy:**
- Pre-position party during dialogue — fight won't trigger until you choose to fight
- **Destroy Soul Pillars first** — Shadowheart with Blood of Lathander (Radiant) melts them
- Once pillars are down, Raphael's damage output collapses
- Fire resistance doesn't help — Ascended form bypasses it

---

### Ansur
**Location:** Wyrm's Rock - Dragon's Sanctum (reached via Wyrmway puzzle)

**Key Mechanics:**
- Undead bronze dragon — Lightning-themed throughout
- Balduran's Gaze: Wide-area Lightning breath — high damage
- Lightning tines: Random Lightning strikes across the arena — spread out, never stand still
- Breath weapon reaction: Retaliates against attacker once per round
- Can fly — gains distance, then lands again

**Immunities:** Lightning (immune or very high resistance), Form-altering, +5 Banishment
**Likely Resistance:** Necrotic (undead)

**Strategy:**
- Equip Lightning resistance on key characters (Sparkswall Ring, Darkfire Shortbow passive, etc.)
- Spread the party across the arena for Balduran's Gaze and tine avoidance
- Don't stack on the same tile — tines hit randomly but clustered parties take multiple hits

---

### Cazador Szarr
**Location:** Cazador's Palace - Dungeon

**Key Mechanics:**
- Ritual counter: Every 3 turns, if not interrupted, Cazador ascends to Vampire Ascendant (absorbs power from Vampire Spawns)
- To interrupt: Free Astarion from the ritual OR kill a Vampire Spawn
- Regeneration: +10 HP per turn — paused by sunlight or Radiant damage
- Mist Form: Immune to physical damage while in Mist Form
- Multiattack: Main hand + two extra attacks

**Immunities/Vulnerabilities:**
- Radiant: Vulnerable (Pelorsun Blade, Blood of Lathander aura)
- Mist Form: Physical damage immunity

**Strategy:**
- Turn 1-3: Kill a Vampire Spawn OR free Astarion to cut the ritual
- Keep Radiant damage up: Shadowheart's Blood of Lathander aura stops regeneration and deals vulnerability damage
- Shadowheart's Sacred Munitions automatically Turns Vampire Spawns (no save) — clears summons fast
- Don't waste physical attacks during Mist Form

---

### Sarevok Anchev
**Location:** Bhaal Temple

**Key Mechanics:**
- Can be ally or enemy depending on Murder Tribunal quest completion
- If Murder Tribunal quest was completed, can be bypassed entirely
- High AC fighter — bring accuracy buffs

---

### Netherbrain (Final Boss)
**Location:** Mindflayer Colony / End Game

**ACHIEVEMENTS:** Crash Landing (hit the flying dragon with arena lightning torches), Interfectorem Draconis (kill the Red Dragon)

**Key Mechanics:**
- Multi-phase fight including Nautiloid assault section
- Crash Landing: Activate wall torches with Lightning during the dragon-riding phase to knock the dragon out of the air (you must be the one to trigger it)
- Interfectorem Draconis: Personally kill the Red Dragon during the final battle

**Note:** Detailed mechanics for Netherbrain's legendary actions and specific phase transitions may vary by patch. Check bg3.wiki for current patch notes.
