# BG3 - Achievement Guide (100%)

**Total Achievements:** 54 (approximate, varies by platform)

**Critical Rules:**
- NO MODS — any mod (including cosmetic, UI, QoL) disables all achievements. Vanilla only.
- Dark Urge required for: Embrace Your Urge, Sins of the Father
- Tactician required for: Critical Hit
- Guide source: Steam Guide #3018760696

## LEAVE NO ONE BEHIND — Full Checklist

Most complex missable. Tracks Tiefling and allied NPC survival across all 3 acts.

### Act 1
- [ ] Convince Rolan to stay with Tiefling refugees (dialogue in Druid Grove)
- [ ] Prevent Aradin from killing Sazza
- [ ] Save Mircon from harpy attack (near riverside)
- [ ] Save Arabella from execution (caught stealing snake idol)
- [ ] Save Nadira from bugbear assassin attack
- [ ] Defend ALL Tieflings during Emerald Grove siege

### Act 2
- [ ] Rescue imprisoned Tieflings (Rolan gives location)
- [ ] **CRITICAL: Prevent Isobel from being kidnapped at Last Light Inn** — if taken, all inn Tieflings die and achievement is permanently broken
- [ ] Help Arabella reunite with parents at House of Healing
- [ ] Save Rolan near the central bridge AFTER rescuing other Tieflings (order matters)
- [ ] Save Zevlor after Ketheric Thorm battle
- [ ] Note Mol's capture — will reunite in Act 3

### Act 3
- [ ] Complete "Save the Tieflings" quest (Danis and Bexom)
- [ ] Prevent Rolan's death
- [ ] Retrieve Mol from the Thieves Guild (Jaheira in party = easy access; or complete Aid the Underduke)

## SHE CANNOT BE CAGED — Full Checklist

- [ ] Act 1: Free Sazza from Emerald Grove prison, prevent Aradin from killing her
- [ ] Act 2: Ensure Sazza survives (she stays in background — don't accidentally kill her in AoE)
- [ ] Act 3: Complete her chain
