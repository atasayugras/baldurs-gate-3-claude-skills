# BG3 - Consumables Reference

All consumables available in BG3: elixirs, potions, grenades, coatings, and special drops. Boss matchup notes included - cross-reference with boss immunities/resistances.

---

## ELIXIRS

Last until long rest. Only one elixir can be active at a time - drinking a new one replaces the old one.

| Name | Effect | Source | Boss / Usage Note |
|------|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Elixir of Arcane Cultivation** | +1 spell slot of highest available level | Alchemy | For casters who have burned through slots |
| **Elixir of Barkskin** | AC 17 (does not stack with armor/shield, but great for unarmored/light armor wearers) | Alchemy | For Druids, Monks, and casters without armor |
| **Elixir of Battlemage's Power** | Arcane Acuity 3 stacks (spell attack +1 / save DC +1 per stack) | Alchemy | Great for Gale against high-save bosses (Raphael, Gortash) |
| **Elixir of Bear's Endurance** | Advantage on CON saving throws + 5 Temp HP | Alchemy | Protects concentration (especially Spirit Guardians / Cloud of Daggers) |
| **Elixir of Bloodlust** | On kill: gain a Bonus Action + 5 Temp HP | Alchemy | For melee DPS in horde encounters (clearing Necromites) |
| **Elixir of Cloud Giant Strength** | STR 27 until long rest | Alchemy (rare ingredients) | Best melee STR buff in the game; devastating attack and damage |
| **Elixir of Colossus** | Character becomes Large, weapon reach +1.5m, Advantage on STR checks | Alchemy | Melee reach situations, large platform fights (Grym) |
| **Elixir of Darkvision** | Darkvision until long rest | Alchemy | For characters without Devil's Sight who need to function in Darkness combos |
| **Elixir of Heroism** | Blessed + Frightened immune | Alchemy | **Priority elixir** - useful before almost every boss fight |
| **Elixir of Hill Giant Strength** | STR 21 | Alchemy | Fallback if Cloud Giant Elixir is unavailable |
| **Elixir of Necrotic Resistance** | Resistance to Necrotic damage | Alchemy | Against Cazador, Apostle of Myrkul, Balthazar |
| **Elixir of Psionic Fortification** | Resistance to Psychic damage + Madness immunity | Alchemy | Against Mind Flayers, Nere, Netherbrain |
| **Elixir of Stone Giant Strength** | STR 23 | Alchemy | Middle option between Hill and Cloud Giant |
| **Elixir of Universal Resistance** | Resistance to all damage types | Alchemy (Divine Bone Shard) | Reserve for extremely dangerous encounters; ingredients are scarce |
| **Elixir of Viciousness** | Critical hits on 19-20 (instead of 20) | Alchemy (Shadowroot Sac) | For melee/ranged DPS to increase burst damage |

| Name | Effect | Source | Boss / Usage Note |
|------|--------|--------|-------------------|
| **Potion of Healing** | 2d4+2 HP | Alchemy / Shop | Emergency healing |
| **Potion of Greater Healing** | 4d4+4 HP | Alchemy / Shop | Mid-combat recovery |

| **Potion of Supreme Healing** | 10d4+20 HP | Alchemy / Shop / Rare drop | Highest heal - last resort |
| **Potion of Speed** | Haste (extra action, +2 AC, Advantage on DEX saves, +9m move) - 3 turns, then Lethargic | Alchemy / Shop | **Priority potion** - use at boss fight opening on DPS or tank. Lethargic = 1 turn no action after expiry, so feed it when the fight is nearly over. **Honor Mode: Haste Potion is nerfed** - only grants Speed + extra move, no extra action |

| **Potion of Gaseous Form** | Gaseous Form (ethereal, free movement) | Alchemy | Escape / passing barriers; not very useful in combat |
| **Potion of Invisibility** | Invisible until you attack or cast | Alchemy / Shop | Opening repositioning |
| **Potion of Fire Resistance** | Resistance to Fire damage (10 turns) | Alchemy / Shop | Against Raphael (ascended form fire spam) |

## SPECIAL / LOOT-ONLY CONSUMABLES

Not craftable; obtained from specific encounters or locations.

### Karabasan's Gift
- **Source:** Act 2 - House of Healing, Dolor drop (near Malus Thorm area), typically 3 drops
- **Throw (Action):** 2m AoE - targets fail CON DC 15 or Paralyzed for 2 turns
- **Drink (Bonus Action):** Delayed 1 turn, then Paralyzed
- **Paralyzed effect:** Incapacitated + Vulnerable to all physical damage (2x) + auto-fail STR/DEX saves + attacks from within 3m auto-hit and auto-crit
- **Boss use:** Save for major boss fights - Great Weapon Master + Extra Attack + auto-crit synergy is devastating

### Karabasan's Poison
- **Source:** Act 2 - same area as the Gift
- **Type:** Weapon coating variant
- **Note:** Despite the name "Poison," bypasses Poison immunity (shadow/dark damage type, not Poison)

### Orthon Explosive
- **Source:** Act 2 - Gauntlet of Shar, Yurgir the Orthon encounter area
- **Type:** Throwable grenade (infernal)
- **Effect:** High damage explosion - Fire + Bludgeoning mix, wide AoE
- **Note:** Rare - can even be used during the Yurgir fight itself since he drops them

## BOSS MATCHUP - IMMUNITY / RESISTANCE QUICK REFERENCE

Always check immunities before using consumables:

| Boss | Immune / Resistant | Avoid Using |
|------|--------------------|-------------|
| **Grym** | All damage (before superheating), Fire/Acid immune | Everything Fire/Acid based |
| **Apostle of Myrkul** | Disease immune, form-altering | Contagion, Polymorph |
| **Apostle Necromites** | Piercing resistant | Serpent Venom, Poisoned Arrow |
| **Balthazar** | Undead - likely Poison/Necrotic resistant | Drow Poison, Noxious Spore |
| **Yurgir** | Infernal - likely Fire resistant | Alchemist's Fire, Oil of Combustion (reduced value) |
| **Ansur** | Lightning immune | Lightning Bottle, Lightning Arrow |
| **Cazador** | Physical immune in Mist Form | Physical coatings wasted during Mist Form |
| **Orin** | Form-altering immune | Polymorph / Wild Shape variants |
| **Raphael (Ascended)** | Fire hits land regardless (even vs fire resist) | Not a concern - all fire hits |
| **All Undead** | Poison immune, disease immune | Drow Poison, Crawler Mucus, Noxious Spore |
| **All Constructs** | Poison immune, disease immune | Same as above |
