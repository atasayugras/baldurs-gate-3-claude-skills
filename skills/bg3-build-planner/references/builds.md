## S-TIER BUILDS

### Sorcadin (Paladin 5 / Sorcerer 7)
- **Difficulty:** Medium (requires understanding nova mechanics)
- **Role:** Nova DPS (Burst damage on boss turns)
- **Honor Mode:** Yes — top tier
- **Core Mechanic:** Sorcery Points convert to spell slots → Divine Smite on crits → huge burst damage. Use Twinned/Quickened Spell for action economy. Extra Attack from Paladin 5 + action surge chains.
- **Key Features:** Divine Smite, Extra Attack, Metamagic (Twinned, Quickened, Heightened), Aura of Protection (+CHA to all saves in party)
- **Leveling Order:** Paladin 1-5 first (Extra Attack, Aura of Protection), then Sorcerer 7
- **Ability Scores:** CHA primary, CON secondary. STR for heavy armor (or DEX if using Dex Paladin variant)
- **Best Race:** Half-Elf (Fey Ancestry), Human, or Zariel Tiefling (extra smite spell)
- **Companions:** Works with almost everything — AoE caster (Gale), Healer, and a melee companion

---

### Darkness Bladelock (Warlock 5 / Fighter 5 or 6 / rest Warlock)
- **Difficulty:** Medium (requires Darkness setup)
- **Role:** Sustained melee DPS with Advantage on demand
- **Honor Mode:** Yes — reliable, not reliant on Haste
- **Core Mechanic:** Cast Darkness on yourself → you're Heavily Obscured (attacks from outside Darkness have Disadvantage vs you) → Devil's Sight from Warlock lets you see through Darkness → you have Advantage, enemies have Disadvantage on all attacks. Extra Attack × 2 + Action Surge.
- **Key Features:** Pact of the Blade (CHA to attack/damage), Devil's Sight, Extra Attack, Action Surge, Eldritch Smite (force damage), Darkness (free from Archfey or gear)
- **Leveling Order:** Warlock 5 first (Pact of Blade + Extra Attack), then Fighter 5 (Extra Attack 2 + Action Surge), then back to Warlock
- **Ability Scores:** CHA primary (attacks + spells), CON secondary (concentration for Darkness), DEX for initiative
- **Key Items:** Any item that grants Darkness (Circlet of Fire, The Shade Stone), War Caster or Resilient CON for concentration
- **Illithid Synergy:** Freecast (free Darkness), Mind Sanctuary (action economy), Fracture Psyche (AC shred)
- **Companions:** Shadowheart (Blood of Lathander for radiant AoE), any AoE caster, frontliner who doesn't rely on sight

---

### Tavern Brawler Monk (Monk 12 or Monk 8 / Thief Rogue 4)
- **Difficulty:** Medium (requires feat + item setup)
- **Role:** High-speed unarmed DPS
- **Honor Mode:** Yes
- **Core Mechanic:** Tavern Brawler feat (STR mod added twice to unarmed attacks) + Monk unarmed strikes as Bonus Action + Thief Rogue's extra Bonus Action. Stack STR to absurd values with items.
- **Key Features:** Tavern Brawler feat, Flurry of Blows, Open Hand Technique (add save effects), Thief's Second Bonus Action
- **Ability Scores:** STR primary (Elixir of Cloud Giant Strength sets STR 27), CON secondary, WIS for AC
- **Key Items:** Elixir of Cloud Giant Strength (BiS elixir every long rest), Gloves of Soul Catching, Amulet of Greater Health, Bracers of Defense
- **Best For:** Karlach as companion

---

### Open Hand Monk / Paladin (Monk 6-9 / Paladin 3-6)
- **Difficulty:** Hard (complex action economy)
- **Role:** Sustained melee DPS with survivability
- **Honor Mode:** Yes — top tier
- **Core Mechanic:** Open Hand Technique procs (stagger, push, prone) on Flurry of Blows + Paladin Aura + Divine Smite on crits. WIS and CHA both contribute to tanky stats.

## A-TIER BUILDS

### Gloomstalker Assassin (Ranger 5 / Rogue 5-7 / Fighter 2-3)
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Role:** First-turn burst DPS — kills priority targets before they act
- **Honor Mode:** Yes — excellent for eliminating key enemies turn 1
- **Core Mechanic:** Gloomstalker adds an extra attack on the first turn + Assassin Rogue crits on surprised enemies + Action Surge = 5-7 attacks on turn 1, all crits if enemies are surprised.
- **Key Features:** Gloomstalker Extra Attack, Assassinate (auto-crit vs surprised), Action Surge, Extra Attack stack
- **Leveling Order:** Ranger 5 (Extra Attack), then Rogue 3 (Assassin), then Fighter 2 (Action Surge), then split remaining levels
- **Ability Scores:** DEX primary, CON secondary
- **Best Race:** Wood Elf (extra movement, Mask of the Wild for stealth)
- **Key Items:** Titanstring Bow (STR mod to ranged attacks), Gloves of Archery, Risky Ring, items with Initiative bonuses

---

### Divination Wizard (Wizard 12)
- **Difficulty:** Easy — excellent for new players
- **Role:** Control and AoE damage
- **Honor Mode:** Yes
- **Core Mechanic:** Portent dice (2 per long rest at lv2, 3 at lv10) replace any attack roll, save, or ability check result. Guarantee a spell lands on a boss or guarantee a boss fails a save.
- **Key Features:** Portent, Arcane Recovery, wide spell list (best in game), Concentration control spells
- **Key Spells:** Hypnotic Pattern, Hold Person/Monster, Ice Storm, Globe of Invulnerability, Wish (if available in mod), Counterspell
- **Companions:** Works with everything — versatile support for any party

---

### Swords Bard (Bard 10 / Fighter 2 or Warlock 2)
- **Difficulty:** Medium
- **Role:** Flexible DPS + party support hybrid
- **Honor Mode:** Yes
- **Core Mechanic:** Slashing Flourish (Blade Flourish) with Ranged or Melee — deal bonus damage and hit multiple targets simultaneously using Bardic Inspiration. Action Surge adds another round of attacks.

## B-TIER BUILDS

### Storm Sorcerer (Sorcerer 12)
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Role:** AoE damage dealer
- **Honor Mode:** Viable
- **Core Mechanic:** Flight for free during combat + call lightning at will + Tempestuous Magic bonus action flight after casting. Incredible mobility and AoE.
- **Key Spells:** Call Lightning, Fireball, Chain Lightning, Haste (careful with Honor Mode nerf), Counterspell

---

### War Cleric Melee (Cleric 2 / Fighter or Paladin X)
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Role:** Frontline tank-healer hybrid
- **Honor Mode:** Yes
- **Core Mechanic:** War Cleric's Channel Divinity (Guided Strike: +10 to hit 1/Short Rest) + heavy armor + healing access + martial weapons. Often runs Life Cleric instead for pure support.

---

### Abjuration Wizard (Wizard 12)
- **Difficulty:** Easy
- **Role:** Tanky support wizard
- **Honor Mode:** Yes
- **Core Mechanic:** Arcane Ward (shield of HP that absorbs damage, refreshes when you cast an Abjuration spell). Effectively doubles your HP pool. Add Counterspell to protect the party.

## COMPANION BUILD RECOMMENDATIONS

### Astarion — Recommended: Gloomstalker 5 / Assassin 3 / Fighter 2 (thematic + strong)
- Ranger 5 → Rogue 3 → Fighter 2 → remaining in Rogue
- First-turn burst + Darkness synergy with Knives of the Undermountain King

### Shadowheart — Recommended: Life Cleric or Trickery Cleric, pure Cleric 12
- Trickery: better for stealthy parties
- Life: strongest sustained healer in the game, Blessed Strikes adds radiant damage to weapons
- Key items: Blood of Lathander (mace), Luminous Armour (radiant proc)

### Lae'zel — Recommended: Battle Master Fighter 12 or Eldritch Knight 12
- Battle Master: Precision Attack (hit more reliably), Trip Attack (prone), Riposte
- Key items: Phalar Aluve (aura buffs entire party), Adamantine Splint Armor (crit immune)

### Karlach — Recommended: Berserker Barbarian 6 / Fighter 4 or Tavern Brawler Monk
- Barbarian: Frenzy for extra attacks, great tanking + damage
- Monk: requires more investment but top-tier

### Gale — Recommended: Evocation or Divination Wizard 12
- Evocation: sculpt spells protects party from AoE
- Divination: Portent dice for guaranteed spell hits

### Wyll — Recommended: Fiend Warlock, Bladelock variant
- Warlock 12 or Coffeelock (Warlock 2 / Sorcerer 10) for spell slot recovery via short rests
- Coffeelock: Short rest Warlock slots → Sorcery Points → Spell slots. Near-infinite casting.

### Halsin — Recommended: Land Druid or Moon Druid (if using)
- Moon: Wild Shape into powerful forms for frontline
- Land: Better spellcasting + bonus cantrips

### Minthara — Recommended: Paladin 12 or Paladin/Warlock
- Full Paladin for reliable aura + smites
- Paladin 5 / Warlock 7 for Cha synergy

### Jaheira — Recommended: Fighter 4 / Druid 8 (her default multiclass is already good)
- Keep her build or respec to Land Druid for more spell slots

### Minsc — Recommended: Ranger 5 / Barbarian 7 or Fighter 6 / Barbarian 6
- High STR tank with Rage resistance + Extra Attack

## HONOR MODE NOTES

- **Haste is nerfed:** Haste Potion gives Speed + extra move only (no extra action). Build plans that rely on Haste potion for double actions are broken on HM.
- **Legendary Actions:** Many bosses gain extra actions. Prepare for longer fights.
- **No resurrect on party wipe:** Keep backline safe.
- **Reliable builds on HM:** Sorcadin, Gloomstalker/Assassin (first-turn kills prevent legendary actions), Divination Wizard (Portent dice guarantee key spell effects)
